The Croissant that Changed Everything

By Your Pen Name · 2025-08-29

It was still warm when the baker slid it across the counter—flaky, golden, suspiciously radiant, like it had opinions about destiny.

I ate it in three bites. On the fourth, the street tilted forty-five degrees and the pigeons started speaking in management jargon.

“We’re pivoting to bread,” one said. “Lots of runway.”

Anyway, that’s how my day went. Yours?
